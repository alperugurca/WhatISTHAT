# WhatISTHAT?
Highlight text to get instant AI explanations. Chrome extension.


Have you ever stumbled upon a complex term, an unknown concept, or a foreign phrase while browsing the web and wished you could instantly know what it is? "What IS THAT?" is your ultimate AI-powered reading companion!

Instead of opening a new tab and breaking your focus, simply highlight any text on any webpage, right-click, and select "What IS THAT?". A beautiful, non-intrusive floating window will instantly appear right next to your cursor, explaining the text using the most powerful AI models in the world.

🚀 26 AI PROVIDERS SUPPORTED
Why limit yourself to just one AI? We support an unprecedented 26 different AI providers. You can choose from:
- OpenAI (ChatGPT)
- Anthropic (Claude)
- Google (Gemini)
- Local Offline AI (Ollama)
- And 22 others including Groq, DeepSeek, Mistral, Cohere, Perplexity, xAI (Grok), Hugging Face, NVIDIA NIM, Alibaba Cloud, Moonshot AI, and more!

✨ INCREDIBLE FEATURES
- Smart Stacking UI: Pin multiple explanations on your screen. They stack neatly underneath each other without overlapping!
- Intelligent Auto-Fetch: Enter your API key and the extension automatically fetches the latest available models for your chosen provider. Just type to search your model!
- Markdown Support: Code blocks, bold text, and lists are formatted perfectly in the results.

🔒 100% PRIVATE & SECURE (BRING YOUR OWN KEY)
Your API keys are saved securely and locally in your own browser's storage. We do not use any intermediary servers, tracking scripts, or databases. Your request is sent directly and securely to the AI provider you select.
